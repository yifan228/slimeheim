## What is MDA?

MDA stands for **Mechanics, Dynamics, and Aesthetics** — a framework for analyzing game design.

### Mechanics
The rules and systems of the game, e.g. jumping, shooting, turn-based combat.

### Dynamics
The behaviors that emerge when players interact with mechanics, e.g. strategy, cooperation, competition.

### Aesthetics
The emotional experience of the player, e.g. challenge, exploration, immersion.
