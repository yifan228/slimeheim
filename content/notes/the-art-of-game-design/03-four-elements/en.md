## The Four Elements of Games

This is the core framework of the book — four elements used to dissect any game.

## Mechanics

The player's goals, rules, and procedures.

## Story

The sequence in which events unfold.

## Aesthetics

The sensory experience — often the most immediate thing a player feels.

## Technology

The medium and methods used to realize the three elements above.
